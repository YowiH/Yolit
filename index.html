Book Notes

▶Context

Characters

  • Jonathan Joestar (JoJo): protagonist, Dio's adoptive brother, a young man squire.
  • Dio Brando: antagonist, a young man who wants every piece of wealth and power in the world.
  • Mary Joestar: posthumous, JoJo's mother, a woman who died in a carriage accident.
  • Dario Brando: Dio's father, a rogue man who stole from George after a carriage accident.
  • George Joestar: JoJo's father, Dio's adoptive father.
  • Erina Pendleton: love interest, a young woman who falls in love with JoJo.

The Stone Mask Prologue

  • Using blood as a catalyst, a man brought a stone mask to life.
  • The mask grants immense power and eternal life by piercing the wearer's brain.
  • The thriving Aztecs of central Mexico mysteriously disappeared from 12th to 16th centure B.C.

Chapter 1: Prologue

  • After a carriage accident, Dario stole from George, but George thought he was being saved.
  • Dario gives Dio a letter for George before dying, and Dio is taken care of by the Joestar family.
  • Jonathan gets beaten trying to save Erina from being bullied and Dio kicks Jonathan's dog.

Chapter 2: Dio Brando the Invader, Part 1

  • Dio strives to be better than Jonathan and make him look bad in everything.
  • Dio bets his monthly allowance to beat Jonathan in boxing and beats him up.
  • Dio tells to everyone that he will teach them boxing moves and makes them hate JoJo.

Chapter 3: Dio Brando the Invader, Part 2

  • Jonathan feels friendless and Erina returns Jonathan his handkerchief in a basket with fruits.
  • Jonathan and Erina have fun until they fall in love, and JoJo carves their names into a tree.
  • Dio sees the heart carving of JoJo and Erina, so he kisses Erina in front of JoJo's old friends.

Chapter 4: Dio Brando the Invader, Part 3

  • Erina washes her face in the gutter and Dio slaps her face.
  • George tells Dio how Danny saved JoJo from drowning and Erina runs out when she sees JoJo.
  • Jonathan finds out what Dio has done and beats him until he cries as revenge.

Chapter 5: Dio Brando the Invader, Part 4

  • The stone mask awakens to Dio's blood, as he cries out to JoJo with a hidden knife.
  • George stops the fight by sending the two to their room, and later Dio incinerates Danny.
  • After Danny's death, Jonathan grieves while unable to speak to Erina, and seven years pass.

Chapter 6: A Letter from the Past, Part 1

  • Jonathan and Dio win a rugby match and rush home to tell their sick dad.
  • Jonathan investigates the stone mask with his archeological notes and discovers Dario's letter.
  • Dio poisons George's medicine, and JoJo asks him about George's symptoms resembling Dario's.

Chapter 7: A Letter from the Past, Part 2

  • Dio bought medicine for Dario, but he told him to buy booze with mother's dress instead.
  • Dio bought poison for George from the same oriental man who sold him poison for Dario.
  • Jonathan takes the poison from Dio and throws him off the top railing to the ground.

Chapter 8: The Stone Mask, Part 1

  • Jonathan tells George not to take any medicine from anyone, as he is going away for three days.
  • Dio breaks into JoJo's drawer and obtains the stone mask, intending to use it to kill JoJo.
  • Jonathan fights off two of three thugs on Ogre Street and asks where they sell eastern poison.

▶Context

Characters

  • Speedwagon: a thug from Ogre Street with a flying saw hat, he fights Jonathan.

Chapter 9: The Stone Mask, Part 2

  • Speedwagon, the third thug, strikes JoJo's arm with his flying saw hat, but JoJo kicks him.
  • Speedwagon recognizes JoJo as a gentleman and offers to help him find the poison dealer.
  • Dio gets in a fight with some hooligans on Ogre Street and tests the stone mask on one of them.

Chapter 10: The Stone Mask, Part 3

  • The stone mask lights up while piercing the wearer's brain.
  • The wearer revives with fangs and incredible power of destruction.
  • The wearer sucks Dio's blood as he gets younger until the sunlight burns him to ash.

Chapter 11: The Stone Mask, Part 4

  • At the Joestar mansion, Dio suddenly meets JoJo, and Speedwagon suggests calling the police.
  • George, the poison seller, and the police suddenly appear from behind the curtain as JoJo moves to handcuff Dio.
  • Dio throws a knife at JoJo, but it hits George instead and Dio puts on the blood-covered stone mask.

▶Context

Characters

  • Yotsuba Koiwai Little girl.
  • Yousuke Koiwai: Father of Yotsuba.
  • Takashi Takeda (Jambo): Friend of Yousuke.
  • Asagi Ayase: Oldest sister.
  • Fuuka Ayase: Middle sister.
  • Ena Ayase: Youngest sister.

Chapter 1: Moving with Yotsuba

  • Yotsuba and Yousuke move in and Yotsuba gets lost when Yousuke and Jambo put the boxes.
  • Fuuka looks for Yotsuba after meeting Yousuke, and Yotsuba runs away from her.
  • Asagi protects Yotsuba from Fuuka; Asagi, Fuuka and Ena welcome Yotsuba and Yousuke.

Chapter 2: Greetings with Yotsuba

  • Yotsuba accidentally breaks the lock of the bathroom and leaves through the window.
  • Finding Yotsuba in her pajamas, Fuuka helps her take care of the house while Yousuke is away.
  • While trying to get out of the bathroom, Fuuka gets stuck in the window and is suddenly found by Yousuke.

Chapter 3: Global Warming with Yotsuba

  • Yotsuba goes to thank Fuuka for drawing her, but finds out she's not there.
  • Asagi shows Yotsuba her cooler and Ena warns her that the coolers contribute to global warming.
  • Asagi tells Yotsuba that coolers are good for the planet because they help to cool it.

▶Context

Characters

  • Nick Carraway: Midwest man pursuing finance, Gatsby's friend, moral center.
  • Jay Gatsby: Mysterious wealthy man in love with Daisy, Nick's neighbor.
  • Daisy Buchanan: Tom's beautiful and selfish wife, Gatsby's love interest.
  • Tom Buchanan: Arrogant old money husband of Daisy.
  • Jordan Baker: Cynical golfer, friend of Daisy.
  • Meyer Wolfsheim: Mysterious gambler with ties to crime, Gatsby's friend.
  • Rosy Rosenthal: Wolfsheim's friend involved in bootlegging, shot at The Old Metropole.

Introduction

  • Nick graduates in New Haven in 1915 and goes to fight in the Great War.
  • Nick goes to New York in 1922 during a summer to learn the bond business.
  • Nick starts living in West Egg (Long Island) next to a rich man's house, the house of Gatsby.

Chapter 1: Tom and Daisy

  • Nick goes to East Egg and has dinner with Daisy, Tom and Jordan.
  • Jordan tells Nick that Tom is having an affair with a woman in New York.
  • Nick returns to West Egg and sees Gatsby stretching his arms to a green light in the sea.

Chapter 2: Myrtle Wilson

  • Tom and Nick go to George's garage, where Tom tells Myrtle to meet in New York.
  • Tom and Myrtle host a party in their apartment, but it turns into an argument between them.
  • Amid the argument, Nick seizes the opportunity to leave and heads back to West Egg.

Chapter 3: I Meet Gatsby

  • Gatsby invited Nick to his party where he meets Jordan.
  • A fellow war veteran approached Nick and scheduled a meeting for the following day.
  • The man revealed himself as Gatsby and disclosed a secret to Jordan.

Chapter 4: I Have Lunch With Gatsby

  • Gatsby takes Nick to have lunch with him and tells him about his sorrow after the loss of his family.
  • At the restaurant, Gatsby introduces Meyer to Nick and they have lunch until Meyer leaves the room.
  • When Nick and Gatsby are leaving, they meet Tom and Gatsby suddenly disappears.

Chapter 5: Daisy and Gatsby: Start of the Dream

  • Jordan tells Nick that Daisy knows Gatsby from five years ago in the Midwest.
  • Daisy had fallen in love with Gatsby, but he didn't meet her family's expectations and she later married Tom.
  • Daisy cried for Gatsby before marrying Tom, but still chose Tom. After hearing about it, Nick kisses Jordan.
