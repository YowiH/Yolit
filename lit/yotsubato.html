Chapter 1: Moving with Yotsuba

Chapter 2: Greetings with Yotsuba

  • Yotsuba accidentally breaks the lock of the bathroom and leaves through the window.
  • Finding Yotsuba in her pajamas, Fuuka helps her take care of the house while Yousuke is away.
  • While trying to get out of the bathroom, Fuuka gets stuck in the window and is suddenly found by Yousuke.

Chapter 3: Global Warming with Yotsuba

  • Yotsuba goes to thank Fuuka for drawing her, but finds out she's not there.
  • Asagi shows Yotsuba her cooler and Ena warns her that the coolers contribute to global warming.
  • Asagi tells Yotsuba that coolers are good for the planet because they help to cool it.
