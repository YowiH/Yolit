Chapter 1: Yotsuba & Moving

  • Yotsuba and Yousuke move in and Yotsuba gets lost when Yousuke and Jambo put the boxes.
  • Fuuka looks for Yotsuba after meeting Yousuke, and Yotsuba runs away from her.
  • Asagi protects Yotsuba from Fuuka; Asagi, Fuuka and Ena welcome Yotsuba and Yousuke.

Chapter 2: Yotsuba & Greetings

  • Yotsuba accidentally breaks the lock of the bathroom and leaves through the window.
  • Finding Yotsuba in her pajamas, Fuuka helps her take care of the house while Yousuke is away.
  • While trying to get out of the bathroom, Fuuka gets stuck in the window and is suddenly found by Yousuke.

Chapter 3: Yotsuba & Global Warming

  • Yotsuba goes to thank Fuuka for drawing her, but finds out she's not there.
  • Asagi shows Yotsuba her cooler and Ena warns her that the coolers contribute to global warming.
  • Asagi tells Yotsuba that coolers are good for the planet because they help to cool it.

Chapter 4: Yotsuba & TV

  • Fuuka gives Yousuke a paper from the neighbourhood association and Yousuke wants to buy furniture
  • After hearing that Yousuke wants a TV, Fuuka offers her old TV to Yousuke and meets Jambo.
  • Yousuke and Jambo carry the TV from the Ayase house to the Koiwai house.

Chapter 5: Yotsuba & Shopping

  • Yousuke and Yotsuba go to a department store and Yousuke buys the missing material for home.
  • Yousuke scares Yotsuba with a bird repellent balloon and the worker warns him for Yotsuba's noise.
  • Yousuke and Yotsuba have a race for an ice cream on the stairs of a Shinto shrine and Yotsuba wins.

Chapter 6: Yotsuba & Cicadas

  • Yotsuba decides to catch cicadas, so she goes with Jambo and Ena to catch some.
  • After returning to the Ayase house, Ena asks Yotsuba for her mom and Jambo says she is adopted.
  • Yotsuba releases all the cicades inside the house and later tells Yousuke about her day at dinner.

Chapter 7: Yotsuba & Rain

  • Fuuka and Yotsuba notice an incoming rain, so Fuuka helps Yotsuba to take the laundry.
  • Fuuka asks Yotsuba about her father's job and Yousuke tells Yotsuba he works as a transporter.
  • Yousuke confirms Yotsuba's adoption to Fuuka while Yotsuba enjoys getting wet in the rain.
