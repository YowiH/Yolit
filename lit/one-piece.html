Characters

  • Monkey D. Luffy

    Young man with a straw hat with a rubber body who cannot swim.

  • Shanks

    Captain of the Red Hair crew and close friend to Luffy.

  • Arubida

    Fat woman with an obsession with beauty and a metal rod.

  • Koby

    Cowardly little boy who has been doing Arubida's chores for 2 years.

  • Roronoa Zoro

    Renowned pirate hunter.

  • Helmeppo

    Spoiled son of Morgan whose wolf was killed by Zoro.

  • Morgan

    Fearful and selfish captain of the Marine with an axe hand.

  • Nami

    Young cunning pirate thief girl.

Pirate prologue

  • Gol D. Roger, king of pirates is sentenced to death.
  • Before dying, he incites everyone to find his treasure, the One Piece.
  • This way, the big great era of pirates arises.

Chapter 1: ROMANCE DAWN - The Dawn of the Adventure

  • Luffy eats Shanks' GomuGomu devil's fruit, turning into gum and unable to swim.
  • Shanks looses his arm to save Luffy from drowning and being eaten by a sea monster.
  • Shanks gives Luffy his straw hat, and ten years later, Luffy conquers the sea monster.

Chapter 2: That Guy, "Straw Hat Luffy"

  • Luffy hides in a barrel during a whirlpool, taken to Arubida's base by Koby.
  • Luffy and Koby try to escape in Koby's boat, but Arubida destroys it.
  • Luffy defeats Arubida and joins Koby to find Roronoa Zoro at the Marine base.

Chapter 3: Introducing "Pirate Hunter Zoro"

  • Luffy and Koby find Zoro tied to a cross in the Marine base and a girl offers Zoro onigiris.
  • Helmeppo steps on the onigiris for having sugar and Zoro asks Luffy to put them on his mouth.
  • Luffy overhears Helmeppo's false promise to free Zoro and punches Helmeppo in the face.

Chapter 4: Marine Captain, "Axe-Hand Morgan"

  • Helmeppo seeks his father's help to kill Luffy, but Morgan refuses and rises a statue of himself.
  • Luffy crushes Morgan's statue and pulls Helmeppo to take him to the location of Zoro's katana.
  • Koby ties off Zoro after realizing he was protecting the village, but someone shoots Koby.

Chapter 5: Pirate King and Master Swordsman

  • Luffy takes the 3 katanas from Helmeppo's room while the Marine surrounds Koby and Zoro.
  • Zoro trained hard to beat Kuina after losing against her, but she died and he took her katana.
  • Luffy covers them so that the bullets bounce off his body and Zoro agrees to be Luffy's crewmate.

Chapter 6: The First

  • Luffy releases Zoro from the ties and sends to fly all the Marine soldiers with a rubber kick.
  • Luffy kicks Morgan's face and Helmeppo uses Koby as a hostage by pointing a gun at him.
  • Luffy punches Helmeppo's face while Zoro scratches Morgan with his swords before he hits Luffy.

Chapter 7: Friends

  • After Zoro faints, he finally eats something in the house of the young girl along Luffy and Koby.
  • The Marines ask the Straw Hat Pirates to leave and Luffy mocks Koby until Koby hits him.
  • Koby joins the Marine and they say goodbye to Luffy and Zoro while they leave on a boat.

Chapter 8: Introducing Nami

  • Luffy is caught by a bird for trying to catch it on the fly and 3 pirates drowning get on board.
  • Nami tricked the 3 pirates with an empty treasure chest and took their ship full of treasure.
  • Nami steals the Grand Line map and Luffy defeats the pirates after they believe he is saving Nami.
