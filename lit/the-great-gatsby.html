Characters

  • Nick Carraway

    Midwest man pursuing finance, Gatsby's friend, moral center.

  • Jay Gatsby

    Mysterious wealthy man in love with Daisy, Nick's neighbor.

  • Daisy Buchanan

    Tom's beautiful and selfish wife, Gatsby's love interest.

  • Tom Buchanan

    Arrogant old money husband of Daisy.

  • Jordan Baker

    Cynical golfer, friend of Daisy.

  • Meyer Wolfsheim

    Mysterious gambler with ties to crime, Gatsby's friend.

  • Rosy Rosenthal

    Wolfsheim's friend involved in bootlegging, shot at The Old Metropole.

Introduction

  • Nick graduates in New Haven in 1915 and goes to fight in the Great War.
  • Nick goes to New York in 1922 during a summer to learn the bond business.
  • Nick starts living in West Egg (Long Island) next to a rich man's house, the house of Gatsby.

Chapter 1: Tom and Daisy

  • Nick goes to East Egg and has dinner with Daisy, Tom and Jordan.
  • Jordan tells Nick that Tom is having an affair with a woman in New York.
  • Nick returns to West Egg and sees Gatsby stretching his arms to a green light in the sea.

Chapter 2: Myrtle Wilson

  • Tom and Nick go to George's garage, where Tom tells Myrtle to meet in New York.
  • Tom and Myrtle host a party in their apartment, but it turns into an argument between them.
  • Amid the argument, Nick seizes the opportunity to leave and heads back to West Egg.

Chapter 3: I Meet Gatsby

  • Gatsby invited Nick to his party where he meets Jordan.
  • A fellow war veteran approached Nick and scheduled a meeting for the following day.
  • The man revealed himself as Gatsby and disclosed a secret to Jordan.

Chapter 4: I Have Lunch With Gatsby

  • Gatsby takes Nick to have lunch with him and tells him about his sorrow after the loss of his family.
  • At the restaurant, Gatsby introduces Meyer to Nick and they have lunch until Meyer leaves the room.
  • When Nick and Gatsby are leaving, they meet Tom and Gatsby suddenly disappears.

Chapter 5: Daisy and Gatsby: Start of the Dream

  • Jordan tells Nick that Daisy knows Gatsby from five years ago in the Midwest.
  • Daisy had fallen in love with Gatsby, but he didn't meet her family's expectations and she later married Tom.
  • Daisy cried for Gatsby before marrying Tom, but still chose Tom. After hearing about it, Nick kisses Jordan.
